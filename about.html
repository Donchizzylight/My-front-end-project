About us

NEW-WORLD DRIVING SCHOOL LIMITED
New-world driving school limited was
incorporated in nigeria with the
corporate affairs commission (CAC) on
11/06/2013 with RC no 1121000.
We are doing everything our pawers and
experiences to help in bringing the high
rate of road tracffic crashes in nigeria to
the barest minimum. For the past 10 years,
we have trained over 10 thousand drivers in
Nigeria. All our instructors are trained by
FESC & DRTS. We have manual and automatic
transmission vehicles in very good condition for
training. Our classroom is equipped with training
facilities like projector, simulator, accident
films, road sign charts, safety tips, first aid box
e.t.c, just to make the environment condusive
for training. Learning driving with us will actually
usher you into a new world of wonderful driving
experainces in life.
